About Ennis Tidy Towns

Ennis Tidy Towns is part of a nationwide community-based competition and movement in Ireland that encourages communities to improve their local environment and make their towns and villages better places to live, work, and visit.

The Tidy Towns Movement

The SuperValu TidyTowns competition has been running since 1958, making it one of the longest-running environmental initiatives in Ireland. It celebrates community involvement, environmental awareness, and the work that local groups put into improving their areas.

Our Mission

Ennis Tidy Towns works year-round to:

  • Keep our town clean, litter-free, and welcoming
  • Create and maintain beautiful floral displays and green spaces
  • Promote environmental sustainability and biodiversity
  • Preserve and enhance our built heritage
  • Foster community pride and civic engagement
  • Support the UN Sustainable Development Goals

Community-Led

We're entirely volunteer-run, made up of local residents who care deeply about our town. Our work is only possible through the dedication, time, and energy of community members like you.

UN Sustainable Development Goals

Tidy Towns groups across Ireland are encouraged to integrate the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) into their work, connecting local action to global objectives.

What are the SDGs?

The 17 Sustainable Development Goals are a universal call to action to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure prosperity for all by 2030. They address global challenges including climate change, environmental degradation, inequality, and peace and justice.

Relevant SDGs for Tidy Towns

Our work directly contributes to several key SDGs:

  • Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities
  • Goal 12: Responsible Consumption and Production
  • Goal 13: Climate Action
  • Goal 14: Life Below Water
  • Goal 15: Life on Land
  • Goal 17: Partnerships for the Goals

Local Action, Global Impact

By participating in Tidy Towns activities, you're not just improving Ennis—you're contributing to a worldwide movement for a more sustainable, equitable future. Every piece of litter picked up, every plant nurtured, and every community member engaged brings us closer to these ambitious goals.

Green Spaces & Planting

Beautiful, well-maintained green spaces make Ennis a more pleasant, healthy place to live. Help us keep our town blooming!

What We Do

  • Maintain floral displays in planters throughout town
  • Care for parks and public gardens
  • Plant trees and native species
  • Create pollinator-friendly habitats
  • Seasonal planting and upkeep

No Experience Needed

Whether you're a seasoned gardener or have never planted a seed, you're welcome! We provide guidance, tools, and all the plants you'll need.

Environmental Benefits

Our green spaces work contributes to:

  • Supporting bees, butterflies, and other pollinators
  • Improving air quality
  • Reducing urban heat
  • Providing habitat for wildlife
  • Enhancing mental health and wellbeing
